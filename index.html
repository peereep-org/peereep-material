// 04 · HOW IT WORKS

Non-Custodial Smart Contracts

payment flow
STEP 1
Buyer Approves Contract
Payment locked in Solana smart contract. Only the buyer (to release) or seller (to receive) can access. Platform has zero access to funds.
STEP 2
Seller Delivers
Seller provides proof of delivery — deliverable, tracking number, or milestone completion.
STEP 3
Buyer Confirms → Instant Release
Smart contract automatically releases USDC to seller. Settlement: ~3–5 seconds.
DISPUTE
Admin Arbitration
Both parties submit evidence. Admin arbitrates. Smart contract executes the decision.
platform cannot
✗ Freeze funds — unlike PayPal, Stripe, or banks
✗ Charge surprise fees — all fees transparent on-chain
✗ Hold payments for months — settlement is instant
✗ Fail and lock customer money — funds never in custody
